About BUSSSA

Our Story

BUSSSA was born from a vision to unite and empower South Sudanese students at Buegema University, creating a home away from home while pursuing academic excellence.

Established in 2020, the Buegema University South Sudanese Students Association (BUSSSA) emerged from the collective desire of South Sudanese students to create a supportive community that would help them navigate their academic journey while maintaining strong connections to their cultural heritage.

What started as informal gatherings of a few students has grown into a vibrant organization that serves as a bridge between South Sudanese students and the broader university community. Our association provides academic support, cultural celebration opportunities, and a strong support network that helps students thrive in their educational pursuits.

Today, BUSSSA stands as a testament to the power of unity and shared purpose. We've successfully implemented various programs including:

  • Peer mentorship programs connecting senior students with freshmen
  • Regular academic support sessions and study groups
  • Cultural festivals celebrating South Sudanese heritage
  • Career development workshops and networking events
  • Community outreach and service initiatives

As we continue to grow, our commitment remains steadfast: to support every South Sudanese student at Buegema University in achieving their academic goals while fostering a strong sense of community and cultural identity. We invite you to be part of our story as we work together to build a brighter future for our members and our nation.

Our Mission

To foster unity, promote academic excellence, and preserve cultural heritage among South Sudanese students at Buegema University while facilitating their personal and professional development.

Our Vision

To be a leading student organization that empowers South Sudanese students to excel academically, professionally, and culturally, while contributing positively to both their host and home communities.

Our History

2020: Foundation

BUSSSA was established by a group of passionate South Sudanese students at Buegema University to create a supportive community.

2021: Growth & Development

Expanded membership and initiated key programs including mentorship and academic support initiatives.

2022: Cultural Integration

Launched annual cultural festivals and established partnerships with other student organizations.

2023: Digital Transformation

Implemented online platforms and resources to better serve our growing membership.
